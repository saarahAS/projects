Prophet Muhammad

A short biography of Prophet Muhammad

Timeline:

  • He was born in Makkah in what is now Saudi Arabia around 570 CE.
  • His father, Abdullāh died before he was born, and his mother died before he was six, so he was first brought up by his paternal grandfather.
  • He was born into the Hāshim clan which was part of the Quraysh tribe.
  • The name Muhammad means “the praised”.
  • He built up a reputation for being trustworthy, truthful and honest, so he was given the nickname, Al-Amīn (The Trustworthy).
  • In 595 CE, when he was 25, he married Khadījah, a wealthy lady, and started a family with her.
  • In 613CE, at the age of 40, the Quran was revealed to him.
  • In the 14th year after receiving Prophethood (622CE), he migrated to Madinah whilst the people of the city eagerly awaited his arrival.
  • The Islamic calendar is dated to this journey of the Prophet from Makkah to Madinah.
  • Muhammad ruled with justice and was loved by the people of Madinah.
  • He died on 8 June 632 AD in Medina, Saudi Arabia.
